Stuck in an Elevator

What to do if you’re stuck in an elevator:

  1. Don’t panic → take deep breaths
  2. Use the call button (all elevators have them)
  3. If no one responds try banging on the door to get the attention of nearby passers.
  4. If help doesn’t come after a while, use the directions below to get out of an elevator.

How to get out of an Elevator:
